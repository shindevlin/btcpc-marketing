# Why BTCPC Matters

## The Problem

### 1. Bitcoin wastes 150 TWh/year on nothing
Bitcoin's network consumes more electricity than many countries — all to produce SHA-256 hashes that serve no purpose except securing the network. The security model works, but the output is pure waste. What if that energy produced something useful?

### 2. AI compute is controlled by 4 companies
OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, and Meta control the vast majority of the world's AI inference capacity. This means:
- **One outage = millions affected** (happened multiple times)
- **Censorship** — they decide what questions can be asked
- **Monopoly pricing** — you pay what they charge, no alternatives
- **Privacy violation** — every prompt passes through their servers

### 3. New blockchains start from zero
Every new blockchain launches with no liquidity, no users, no ecosystem. They spend millions on exchange listings and market makers. There's no organic way to bootstrap value on multiple chains simultaneously.

## The Opportunity

BTCPC solves all three at once:

### Useful Mining
Every unit of energy spent mining BTCPC produces real AI inference that people pay for. The work that secures the network IS the work that serves users. No wasted energy.

### Decentralized AI
Anyone with a GPU can be an AI provider. No single company controls access. If one node goes down, others take over. The network is censorship-resistant — nobody can prevent a computation from happening.

### Cross-Chain Liquidity
When a miner earns BTCPC, they automatically receive claimable tokens on every blockchain they're connected to — Ethereum, Solana, Bitcoin, TON, Hive. One chain generates liquidity across the entire crypto ecosystem from day one.

## Why Now

- **AI demand is exploding** — inference costs are the fastest-growing line item in tech
- **GPU hardware is widely available** — anyone can buy a GPU and start mining
- **Environmental pressure on crypto** — regulators and the public want mining that does something useful
- **Centralization of AI is accelerating** — the window to build decentralized alternatives is closing
- **Open-source AI models are production-ready** — qwen, llama, mistral can run on consumer hardware
