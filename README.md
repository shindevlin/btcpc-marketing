## Who Is This For

This handoff is designed for a **non-technical marketing company** that may not have blockchain or AI expertise. Every document is written in plain English. Technical details are included only where they strengthen the marketing story.

## The Elevator Pitch

> Bitcoin proved that a decentralized network can create digital scarcity. But Bitcoin's mining produces only security — no secondary output. BTCPC applies the same model to AI — miners earn tokens by providing real, useful AI compute to the network. Every unit of energy spent mining BTCPC produces something people actually want: AI inference. It's Bitcoin, but the work matters.
