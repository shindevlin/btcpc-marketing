## Emission Schedule

BTCPC uses a doubling-then-halving emission model:

| Period | Duration | BTCPC Mined | Reward/Epoch |
|--------|----------|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | 1 month | 2,100,000 | ~243 |
| 2 | 2 months | 2,381,400 | ~137 |
| 3 | 4 months | grows... | decreases... |
| ... | ... | ... | ... |
| Total | ~11 periods | 42,000,000 | - |

Empty epochs (no AI work done) don't consume reward slots — the emission schedule stretches. This means the actual mining duration could be much longer than the periods suggest.

## What NOT to Say

- Don't call it a "Layer 2" — it's a sovereign chain
- Don't call it a "token on Ethereum" — BTCPC is its own blockchain
- Don't compare the AI quality to GPT-4 — the models are smaller but decentralized
- Don't promise specific returns — mining rewards depend on network activity
- Don't say "zero fees" — there are small inference fees (that's the business model)
- Don't say "anonymous" — accounts have public keys on-chain (pseudonymous, not anonymous)
